Detailed Project Description

Visualization control platform

The construction of a digital twin visualization management and control platform for smart mines is an intelligent management and control platform with global perception, edge computing, data-driven and intelligent decision-making. The platform integrates technologies such as the Internet of Things, cloud computing, big data and AI to achieve virtual-real mapping and real-time interaction between physical mine entities and digital mine twins. At the same time, it can perform three-dimensional visualization of production scenes, real-time monitoring of equipment performance, remote intelligent operation of production processes, and early warning of accidents such as gas over-limit and water inrush precursors. By utilizing the multi-dimensional integration of mine operation and maintenance data, deep learning, iterative optimization and autonomous decision-making, the intelligence of the entire life cycle of mine operation and maintenance services can be realized.

Electrical Intelligent Control System

The mine distributed electrical intelligent control system is a system that integrates advanced information technology, communication technology and automation control technology, aiming to improve the safety, efficiency and intelligence level of coal mines. In the project, we established a mine power system model, a mine control system model and a mine monitoring system model. The mine power system model includes four main areas: the mine ground substation area, the landscape substation area, the underground substation area and the mining area substation area, which are mainly implemented through efficient interconnection through distribution lines. In the mine control system, production efficiency is improved by optimizing the mine production parameters, and the model of the mine production parameter printing adopts frequency conversion calculation, deep learning and other calculation methods to change the dynamic factors, and finally gives a real-time update method driven by big data.

Three-machine joint control

Joint control of coal mining machine, scraper conveyor and hydraulic support

This project mainly optimizes the action formula of the three machines to plan the linkage control model and strategy, realize the adaptive adjustment and matching of control parameters, so as to achieve the purpose of coordinated control, and finally realize the automation and unmanned operation of the comprehensive mining face.

Intelligent control of speed and tension of mine belt conveyor

Belt conveyor, also known as belt conveyor, has been widely used in modern high-yield and high-efficiency mines due to its large transportation capacity, strong transportation capacity, long transportation distance, strong adaptability and stable operation. With the development needs of coal intelligence, belt conveyor, as the key equipment of the main coal transportation system, its intelligence level directly determines the key efficiency of coal and the level of coal intelligence construction. Traditional belt conveyors mostly run at rated speed in actual production. However, in production, the conveyor is not always fully loaded. When the load of the conveyor is reduced, the conveyor always runs at rated speed, which will inevitably cause a waste of kinetic energy. Based on this, we divide this project into independent control and joint control and active control and tension control. The energy-saving technology for optimizing the startup process of the belt conveyor system is to optimize the startup sequence of multiple conveyors.

Development of intelligent control system for mine hoists

This project will use a friction floor hoist, which has the advantages of strong stability, convenient maintenance and good seismic performance. Its working principle is based on the friction between the wire rope and the friction lining wheel. During the lifting process, the wire rope is placed on the friction wheel, and the friction wheel rotates through the motor, and then the friction between the wire rope and the friction lining is used

Security monitoring

Safety detection of electromechanical equipment

Traditional fault detection of electromechanical equipment in mines has problems such as high labor intensity, low detection efficiency, and high risk factor. In order to avoid the above problems, we optimize this. By analyzing the common fault types and traditional detection in mines, machine learning is used to identify, identify features and diagnose, and a diagnostic solution for intelligent detection in mines is proposed. Taking electromechanical equipment as the receptor, the operation data of mine electromechanical equipment is collected to obtain the operation status of the equipment, and the abnormal operation data is feature extracted, and after signal processing, it becomes an effective fault signal. The trained network model is used for classification discussion to obtain the fault type of the equipment and propose a fault handling solution.

Safety detection of mine personnel behavior

Because in the process of mine production, due to the complexity of the environment, the danger of production equipment, and the closed nature of the workplace, mines become high-risk places. In order to effectively improve the level of mine safety management and reduce the occurrence of safety accidents, this module mainly implements the following three functions: personnel positioning, personnel behavior safety detection, personnel behavior analysis, and safety warning. For the research objectives and methods of this project, I aim to develop a smart mine personnel behavior safety detection system based on CNN and OpenPose technology, which can detect the location of personnel in real time, and analyze and warn their safety behavior.

New Energy Mining Truck

New Energy Mine Car Project The mine car is driven by electricity and is mainly used for the transportation of coal ores in the mine. It has the characteristics of energy saving and environmental protection, reduces the pollution emissions of traditional fuel mine cars, and makes outstanding contributions to green mines. It is mainly divided into several modules: perception system, prediction control system, Max of vehicle networking, and vehicle design. In this autonomous driving system, the mine car is equipped with laser radar, millimeter wave radar and camera, which can achieve path recognition and obstacle detection, ensuring that the vehicle can operate efficiently in complex mines. The whole vehicle is also equipped with autonomous driving functions, pedestrian monitoring and other functions. When the mine car is running, it can identify different paths and select the optimal path to realize operation. With the help of the signal base station, it can locate the overall position of all mine cars, making the transportation system of the entire mine safer and more efficient.
