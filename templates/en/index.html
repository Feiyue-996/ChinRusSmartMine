WELCOME TO OUR HOMEPAGE



我们项目的名字叫做智慧矿山，我们通过深度融合人工智能、自动控制、工业物联网等技术，设计出一个全面感知、实时互传、分析决策、自主学习、动态预测、协同控制的矿山智慧系统。 从而实现煤炭的勘探、开采、运输、分拣、安全保障、生产管理等全过程的智能化运行。创建出一套完整的数字孪生模型，并开发一个软件平台，在不影响实际生产的情况下进行智能控制、分析和评估。


The name of our project is Smart Mine. By deeply integrating artificial intelligence, automatic control, industrial Internet of Things and other technologies, we have designed an intelligent mining system with comprehensive perception, real-time transmission, analytical decision-making, autonomous learning, dynamic prediction and collaborative control.This will enable intelligent operation of the entire process of coal exploration, mining, transportation, sorting, safety assurance, and production management. Create a complete digital twin model and develop a software platform for intelligent control, analysis and evaluation without affecting actual production.
