学校介绍

School-Introduction

Saint Petersburg Polytechnic University, also known as Peter the Great St. Petersburg Polytechnic University, is a renowned public engineering university located in Saint Petersburg. Founded on February 19, 1899, by notable figures including Dmitri Mendeleev, it has a rich history and a strong academic reputation. The university is part of the 5-100 Project, aimed at enhancing the global competitiveness of Russian universities. It is led by An-Yi Lushkevich and boasts a faculty that includes 25 academicians from the Russian Academy of Sciences and the Russian Academy of Engineering. Additionally, 57 members of the Russian Academy of Engineering and the Russian Academy of Educational Sciences are affiliated with the institution.SPbPU has a distinguished list of alumni, including Nobel Prize winners such as Semyon Semenov, Pyotr Kapitsa, and Lev Landau, who have either studied or worked at the university. Other notable alumni encompass a wide range of fields, with names like Gao Jingde, Ni Weidou, Fu Hengzhi, Salatyn Murtaza, Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, Kapiça, Mikhail Botvinnik, Mikhail Ilyich Kalashnikov, Oleg Yegorov, and many others contributing to the university's legacy. With a focus on excellence in education and research, Saint Petersburg Polytechnic University continues to be a leading institution in the field of engineering and technology.

教授介绍

Professor

кожубаев юрий нургалиевич

Yu.N. Kozhubaev graduated in 2006 from the St. Petersburg State Mining Institute named after Plekhonov (Technical University) with a degree in "Electric Drive and Automation of Industrial Equipment and Technological Complexes." In 2016, he defended his Ph.D. dissertation at St. Petersburg Polytechnic University named after Peter the Great. The academic degree of Candidate of Technical Sciences in the specialty 05.09.03 "Electrotechnical Complexes and Systems" was awarded in 2017 after completing correspondence postgraduate studies at the aforementioned university. In 2018, he obtained a second higher education in the field of "Linguistics" (English, French language) at St. Petersburg Polytechnic University named after Peter the Great. In 2022, he underwent professional retraining in the program "Mathematical Modeling and Information Technologies" at the ANO DPO "Institute of Modern Education." In 2024, he underwent professional retraining in the programs: "Technosphere Safety in the profile 'Fire Safety', 'Environmental Protection and Ecological Safety', 'Labor Protection'" at the ANO DPO "Institute of Modern Education." Yu.N. Kozhubaev has extensive practical experience working in the real sector of the national economy. He began his production activities as an electrician of the 3rd category in mining companies of the Pechora coal basin in Vorkuta, then as a general worker of dormitory No. 4 of the Mining University. After graduating from the Mining University, he gained significant practical experience working in engineering and technical positions in the industry (leading engineer for mining works, mechanic of lifting stationary equipment, mechanic of the underground section, head of industrial, fire, and environmental safety services; head of production control department, deputy chief engineer; deputy director) in industrial enterprises, coal mines, quarries for the extraction of gravel, and organizations for the construction of the metro. He worked in state civil service as a state inspector of the department of supervision over the safety of mining works of the North-West Directorate of the Federal Service for Ecological, Technological and Nuclear Supervision (Rostekhnadzor). The total work experience in the industry is 16 years. Yu.N. Kozhubaev has been working as an associate professor since 2017 and has over 30 scientific publications, including research articles, textbooks, patents of the Russian Federation, and a certificate of the Russian Federation for state registration of a computer program. The results of his research work have been used in industrial enterprises. He has been thanked by the Minister of Transport of the Russian Federation (Order No. 20/n of February 15, 2024). He has also been thanked by the Chairman of the Committee on Territorial Development of St. Petersburg (Order No. 18-rk of July 8, 2020).
