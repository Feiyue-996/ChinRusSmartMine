Project Applications

The system is one of the core components of an intelligent mine. By automating and intelligentizing the electrical control of all types of equipment in the mine, the system can achieve remote operation, real-time monitoring and automatic alarm functions. This system utilizes state-of-the-art sensors and controllers to ensure that all equipment operates at its optimal level. The Electrical Intelligent Control System is also self-diagnostic, allowing it to quickly pinpoint the problem when equipment malfunctions occur. and sends alerts to maintenance personnel, minimizing equipment downtime and increasing productivity.









Safety detection systems are an important part of safeguarding the The safety detection system is an important link to guarantee the life safety of mine operators. Through the sensors arranged in each area of the mine, the system can monitor environmental parameters such as gas concentration, dust concentration, temperature and humidity in real time. Once the value exceeding the safe range is detected, the system will immediately issue an alarm and activate the corresponding alarms. The system will immediately issue an alarm and activate the corresponding emergency plan. In addition, the safety detection system can track and manage the location of workers, ensuring that they can be quickly located and rescued in the event of an emergency.
